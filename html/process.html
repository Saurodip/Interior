step to design success

A Beautiful, Successful Interior Does Not Happen By Accident; those perfect rooms are the result of intention, planning, calculation, listening, understanding, relationship, and experience. The rooms that look timeless, that quickly become your favourite, are achieved through a process of developing size, scale, proportion, colour and light.

conversation

We'll meet in our showroom to get to know you, your house, your needs, and your vision.

plan view

We determine the optimal objects for and layout of the room and come up with proposal.

design presentation

The designer will assemble all of the samples that will allow you to ‘picture’ the room as it will appear when completed.

final delivery

Everything you are expecting, will be delivered and setup on the same day, after the completion of any preliminary site work.

As your room comes together, you will see that our diligence up until this step has really paid off – your room looks and feels even better than you had imagined.
